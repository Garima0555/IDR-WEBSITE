ABOUT IDR

Industry-Led
Training for a Risk-Defined World

The Institute for Digital Risk is an industry-led training and deployment institute purpose-built for digital, cyber and technology risk roles in regulated, high-consequence environments.

We operate at the intersection of academia and industry — partnering with leading UK universities to ensure our programmes are grounded in the latest research while remaining directly applicable to real-world challenges.

✔ Partnership with leading UK universities
✔ Practice-oriented, not just theoretical
✔ Built for regulated, high-consequence sectors
✔ From training to deployment — end to end
